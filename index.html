I enjoy building products that empower people and make a positive impact on the world.
I'm the Manager of Revenue Operations and Product at Hometeam, a startup that provides home healthcare for older adults. Our unique approach involves empowering great caregivers with career growth and giving peace of mind to families through increased transparency. I work on our internal tools team where I helped design our invoicing, payroll, and payment management tools. I also spearheaded the development of our insurance billing product, which automates the generation of caregiver activity logs.

Before Hometeam, I worked at Handy, an on-demand home services startup based in New York. It helped people book cleaners or handymen instantly through the website or mobile app. I joined when there were 6 team members and as a project manager took on many responsibilities in operations, marketing, recruiting, and user testing.
